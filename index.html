  1. Gather your bread, peanut butter, jelly, and a knife!
  2. Lay two pieces of bread flat on the counter.
  3. Slather one slice with peanut butter.
  4. Slather the second slice with jelly.
  5. Smush them together.
  6. Use your knife to slice the sandwich DIAGONALLY!
